> **Who is this for?** Every photographer, studio owner, and client using Pixova.

## What is Pixova?

Pixova is a **business management app built for Indian photographers**.

Think of it like having a full-time assistant who:
- Tracks all your bookings and clients
- Sends payment requests to clients on WhatsApp
- Collects digital signatures on agreements
- Delivers photo galleries to clients
- Sends automatic reminders — all without you doing anything manually

**Everything happens on WhatsApp.** No app download needed for your clients.

## Step 1 — How Photographers Sign Up (2-Step Verification)

This is how Pixova keeps your account 100% secure — no passwords needed.

### Step 1.1 — Enter Your Phone Number

```
Open pixova.in → Click "Get Started"
→ Enter your mobile number (e.g. 9876543210)
→ Click "Send OTP"
```

### Step 1.2 — Verify via WhatsApp OTP

```
You get a WhatsApp message from Pixova:
"Your Pixova OTP is 482910. Valid for 5 minutes."
→ Enter the 6-digit OTP on the screen
→ You're logged in instantly
```

**Why this is better than password login:**
- No password to remember or forget
- No one can hack your account without your phone
- Works even if you change phones — just re-verify your number
- OTP expires in 5 minutes — extra security

## Step 2 — Photographer Onboarding (First Time Setup)

After first login, Pixova walks you through a quick setup — takes less than 3 minutes.

```
Step 1: Studio name + your photo
Step 2: Specializations (wedding, portrait, commercial...)
Step 3: City + years of experience
Step 4: Your UPI ID (Google Pay / PhonePe ID — for receiving payments)
Step 5: Done ✅
```

After this, your photographer profile is live on Pixova's Explore page where clients can find you.

## Step 3 — Creating a Booking

When a client confirms with you, add them to Pixova in 60 seconds.

```
Dashboard → New Booking
→ Enter client name + phone number
→ Select event type (Wedding / Engagement / Portrait...)
→ Pick event date and venue
→ Set your package (price, hours, deliverables)
→ Save
```

**What happens automatically:**
- Client gets a WhatsApp: *"Hi Priya, your booking with Ravi Studio is confirmed for 15 June 2026..."*
- Booking appears on your Calendar
- Payment tracker starts at ₹0 received

## Step 4 — Sending Payment Requests

No more awkward "please pay the advance" calls. Pixova handles it.

### How it works:

```
Bookings → [Client Name] → Payments → "Send Payment Request"
→ Enter amount (e.g. ₹10,000 advance)
→ Your UPI ID is pre-filled (from your profile)
→ Click "Send via WhatsApp"
```

**What the client receives on WhatsApp:**

```
Hi Priya 👋

Ravi Studio has sent you a payment request of ₹10,000
Booking Ref: PX0012-2026

Pay here: upi://pay?pa=ravi@oksbi&am=10000...
[QR Code image]

Scan the QR or tap the link to pay via Google Pay, PhonePe, or Paytm.
```

**Money goes directly to your bank account.** Pixova is not in the middle.

### After client pays:

```
Client pays via their UPI app
→ Money hits your bank instantly
→ You go to Pixova → Record Payment → enter amount
→ Payment history updated
→ Client gets WhatsApp receipt
```

## Step 5 — Sending the Agreement

Protect yourself legally — get clients to sign before the shoot.

```
Bookings → [Client] → Agreement → "Send Agreement"
→ Agreement generated with booking details pre-filled
→ Client gets WhatsApp: "Your agreement is ready to sign"
→ Client opens link → reads → signs digitally (no download needed)
→ Signed agreement saved in Pixova for both parties
```

## Step 7 — Delivering the Gallery

After editing, share photos securely with a private link.

```
Bookings → [Client] → Gallery → Upload Photos
→ Upload edited photos (up to your plan storage limit)
→ Click "Publish Gallery"
→ Client gets WhatsApp with private gallery link
```

**What the client sees:**
- A beautiful gallery page at `pixova.in/g/[unique-link]`
- No login, no app download — just opens in browser
- Can download their photos
- Link is private — only people with the link can see it

## Step 8 — Subscribing to Pixova

Photographers pay for their Pixova plan using UPI — the same way they use Google Pay daily.

```
Settings → Subscription → Choose Plan
→ Modal opens with QR code (Pixova's UPI ID)
→ Scan with Google Pay / PhonePe → Pay ₹999 / ₹1999 / ₹4999
→ Enter UTR number (transaction reference from your UPI app)
→ Submit
→ Pixova team verifies and activates within a few hours
→ You get WhatsApp: "Your Professional plan is now active! 🎉"
```
